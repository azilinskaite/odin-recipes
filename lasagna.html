Vegetarian lasagna

Description

A veggie lasagna is comforting, flavorful, and great for making ahead. I'm sharing my favorite vegetarian lasagna recipe below. It's savory and satisfying, filled with roasted veggies, creamy ricotta cheese, ruffly noodles, and plenty of marinara sauce. I think you're going to love it!

Ingredients:

  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 zuchinni
  • 1 onion
  • 200g of spinach
  • lasagna pasta
  • 200g marinara sauce
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • shredded pecorino
  • fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Roast the veggies. Toss the peppers, zucchini, onions, and mushrooms with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake at 175°C until they're tender and caramelized.
  2. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  3. Make the ricotta filling. Mix the ricotta cheese with the lemon zest, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the tomato sauce on the bottom.
  5. Then, add a layer of pasta.
  6. Top the noodles with half the ricotta mixture.
  7. Layer half the spinach over the ricotta, and scatter half the roasted vegetables in an even layer.
  8. Dot the vegetables with 2/3 cup of the marinara sauce.
  9. Repeat the layering process in the same order.
  10. Top with a final layer of noodles, the remaining sauce, and the shredded cheese.
  11. Bake uncovered at 175°C for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is browned and bubbling.
